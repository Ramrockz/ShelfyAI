Common Questions

Everything you need to know about ShelfyAI.

How does the AI scan my order screenshots? ▼
Our AI uses Computer Vision to identify product names, quantities, and dates from your images. It then cross-references these with your "Recipes" to automatically deduct the correct amount of ingredients from your inventory.
Do I need to update my stock manually after an invoice? ▼
Not unless you want to! When you upload a supply receipt, ShelfyAI gives you the option to "Sync to Stock." If you haven't physically received the items yet, you can save the expense and sync the stock later with one click.
Is my business data secure? ▼
Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption for all data and images. Your financial information and recipe secrets are only visible to you.
What happens if the AI matches the wrong recipe? ▼
ShelfyAI is a "Human-in-the-loop" system. Before any inventory changes are applied, we show you the match. If it's incorrect, you can select a different recipe from your library manually.
Can I export my data for tax season? ▼
Yes! Pro users can export all Sales, Expenses, and Inventory reports in CSV or PDF format, making it easy to share with your accountant.
